Company Overview

We don't build businesses. We build institutions.

NKM GLOBAL (Pty) Ltd is a diversified investment and business development company founded in July 2020. Headquartered in Pretoria, the company is 100% black youth-owned and committed to sustainable economic growth across Africa

We build institutions that outlast individuals, create generational wealth, and transform the economic landscape of the continent. We are currently reactivating NKM GLOBAL from the ground up, with critical minerals beneficiation as our foundational entry point, which is not a limitation, but a foundation.

Chairman's Message
“We are not building businesses. We are building institutions. A business serves its founder, whereas an institution serves its purpose, its people, and its continent long after its founder has stepped aside.
Neo Kgolane-Jr Mahlobogoane
Founder, CEO — NKM GLOBAL (Pty) Ltd

I founded NKM GLOBAL in 2020, at the age of 18, believing Africa's economic transformation will be built by Africans, on African soil, with African capital and African leadership.

We are starting with critical minerals beneficiation because Africa's wealth has been extracted for too long. It is time we processed it, traded it, and benefited from it on our own terms.

"NKM GLOBAL is not a promise. It is a structure being built, one day at a time."

Frequently asked questions
